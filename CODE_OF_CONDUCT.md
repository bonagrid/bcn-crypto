# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitments
As contributors and creators of this project, we pledge to make participation open to everyone, regardless of age, gender, race, experience level, or religion.

## Our Standards
* Use welcoming and inclusive language.
* Respect diverse viewpoints.
* Accept code criticism constructively.
* Focus on what's best for the community.

## Moderator Responsibilities
The lead author has the right to delete comments, close pull requests, and ban participants for toxic behavior or violations of project standards.
